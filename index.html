    Learning about Marketing

    Christy Park was born in Pittsburgh, PA, USA. She grew up in Vancouver, Canada. She currently lives in Seoul, Korea. She went to high school in Connecticut, a boarding school known for its history and culture. She went to New York City to study, but dropped out before her final semester to pursue her diverse interests. She was an economics major while in college, but was unsatisfied with her prospects and was not immersed in the studies. She found interest in marketing and public relations while interning and a PR and advertising company.
