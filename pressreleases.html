    • Famed American Design Company Goes Abroad

    Luxury home design specialists expand their business to Asia

    City, State: Luxuryhomes.com, the leading home design company in North America today announced it would be expanding its operations to Asia. The company, which prides itself on modeling and producing striking and unique designs for residential and commercial projects, will tie up with Taozi Construction to expand its business.

    “We are absolutely thrilled to partner with Taozi Construction and show the world that our designs are truly the best, not only in the States but across the world,” says owner and CEO of Luxury Homes, Larry Ward. “Their contribution in every project will be significant, and it’s an honor to be able to design alongside their team.”

    Luxuryhomes.com has been designing state-of-the art facilities and private homes for North American clientele since 2009. The company revealed although Taozi Construction has already integrated the company’s software and sales tools as of November of this year, that in the time given, the designs would continue to be developed in Los Angeles.

    Luxury homes originally started as an independent project in 2009 to serve celebrity clientele. Today they have a record of designing over four hundred luxury houses and office spaces that have all sold on the market at a high value. They continue to excel in the art and craft of in-depth design. Visit the website at www.luxuryhomes.com.
