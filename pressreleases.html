    Ever got stuck in the mud and can't get out? That's how I felt trying to put the pieces of my life together. I didn't have a breakdown or anything like that but I was in a limbo: I did not know where I was going, or where to start. I only knew one thing: that I had to make a drastic change in my life or I would be gobbled up by the rest of the world. My safe haven was writing, and it has been for a long time. But these past two years have brought nothing but distractions from my ultimate goal.

    Today I would start writing again, and I would most importantly continue learning about marketing. It would help me become more grounded and authentic. It would keep me on my toes.
