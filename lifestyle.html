I went to the hospital today. I was armed with a bag and my Macbook and my coffee tumbler. Everyone should have a coffee tumbler for when they go somewhere earlier in the morning, for something simple like a hospital trip, for example. You can replace the coffee with hot tea. I love how simple things can make your day much better, or more bearable. I usually detest hospital visits, but having coffee with me made me happier. Another item you must not miss is a nice bag to carry your Macbook in. I prefer black bags because they go with anything and draw less attention. I'm into monotone colors. In fact I recently discovered a online shoe store called Monobabie. Their shoes are excellent and within a reasonable price range. Here's their website: Monobabie. Any chance I get I try to wear monotone colors. Anyhow this bag from Zara fits my laptop perfectly and is very sturdy.
